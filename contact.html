    Contact page

    The multifluid Fluidity/IC-Ferst code base is developed and used at a number of academic institutions across the UK, including Imperial College London, the University of Aberdeen and London South Bank University. The code is released under the GNU Lesser Public License version 2.1. Public releases are made at irregular intervals, with internal releases on a monthly cycle.

    To request access to the development repository, or to request further information, please contact the lead developer, Professor Christopher Pain.
