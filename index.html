    IC-FERST

    IC-FERST is a next generation reservoir simulator based on the Double-Control-Volume Finite Element method, dynamic unstructured mesh optimisation and surface-based modelling for the geological representation. IC-FERST is free and open-source (AGPLv3).

    Surface based modelling

    Petrophysical properties are represented mesh free by using NURBS surfaces.
